What is Spacecraft

A SPACECRAFT IS A MAN-MADE VEHICLE DESIGNED FOR TRAVEL OR OPERATION IN OUTER SPACE.

Spacecraft were not invented by a single individual; rather, their development involved contributions from various scientists, engineers, and organizations over time, with pioneers like Konstantin Tsiolkovsky and Robert Goddard playing significant roles.
